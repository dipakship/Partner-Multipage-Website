The Wolf and the Seven Little Kids

Story Adapted from Jacob and Wilhelm Grimm's Original

Retold by Dipakshi and Alsiddig

This is a simplified retelling of the children's fairy tale "The Wolf and the Seven Little Kids" originally published in the book "Grimm's Fairy Tales" in 1812 by the Grimm brothers.

We hope you enjoy our (illustrated) retelling of this wonderful fairytale!
